Our Story

Computer 4 Kids was born from a simple observation: many young children today grow up with tablets and phones but lack the fundamental computer skills needed for school and future careers. Traditional typing programs were boring, and comprehensive computer education for young learners was hard to find.

We set out to change that by creating an engaging, safe, and comprehensive platform that makes learning computer skills feel like play. Every lesson is designed with input from educators, parents, and children to ensure it's both effective and enjoyable.
