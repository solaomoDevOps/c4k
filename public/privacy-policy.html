Computer 4 Kids is committed to protecting the privacy and safety of children. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and take extra care to ensure a safe learning environment.

1. Information We Collect

Parent/Guardian Information:

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)

Child Profile Information:

  • Child's first name only (no last names required)
  • Avatar selection (emoji character)
  • Learning progress and achievements
  • XP points and completed lessons

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device type and browser information
  • Usage patterns and interactions with lessons
  • Session data for improving user experience

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide personalized learning experiences
  • Track educational progress
  • Improve our educational content and features
  • Communicate important updates to parents
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse

3. Information Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell or rent any user information
  • Share children's data with third-party advertisers
  • Display targeted advertising to children
  • Allow social media sharing from children's accounts

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Restricted access to user data

5. Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review their child's information
  • Request deletion of their child's data
  • Refuse further collection of their child's data
  • Update or correct information at any time

6. COPPA Compliance

Computer 4 Kids is designed for children ages 4-12 and complies with COPPA requirements. We obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information from children under 13.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for:

  • Maintaining login sessions
  • Saving user preferences
  • Analyzing site usage (anonymized data only)

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

8. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Parents can request account deletion at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data within 30 days.

9. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Supabase (secure database hosting)
  • Google OAuth (optional secure authentication)

These services comply with strict privacy standards and do not use children's data for their own purposes.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Parents will be notified of significant changes via email. Continued use of the platform after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your child's data:

Email: privacy@computer4kids.com

Response Time: We respond to privacy inquiries within 48 hours.
